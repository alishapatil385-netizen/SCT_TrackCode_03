# SCT_TrackCode_03

Dashboard Insights
Employees aged 26–35 show the highest attrition.
Research & Development department has the highest employee count and attrition contribution.
Sales Executive and Research Scientist roles experience higher attrition compared to other roles.
Employees in lower salary slabs are more likely to leave the organization.
Overtime has a significant impact on employee attrition.
